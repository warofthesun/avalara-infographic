Streamlining Compliance for All Customer Types

If your business serves both taxable and tax-exempt customers, Avalara helps simplify the process. With Avalara, exempt customers can submit certificates at checkout, automatically applying valid exemptions at the time of sale. This not only ensures compliance but also enhances the experience for all customers by reducing potential delays and minimizing audit risks.
